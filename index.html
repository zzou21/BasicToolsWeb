To install Basic Tools, your computer needs to have at least 400 MB of storage space. Because Basic Tools uses Electron, it is not a small app (see why) and will take around 200-370-ish MB of your space. The benefit is that Basic Tools runs completely locally without internet connection, providing security, digital privacy, and a way to not rely everything on internet-based or AI-based tools.

Because Basic Tools runs locally and isolated from the interne, your app cannot detect newly released updates. To keep up-to-date and enjoy the latest version of Basic Tools, please subscribe to this mailing list here.

Want to contribute your ideas/suggestiosn to Basic Tools? Visit the "Forum" page on the menu bar above and share in your thoughts!

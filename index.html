I use Mac but not sure whether I should download the Apple Silicon or Intel version? Read here.

For a smooth downloading process, please ensure that your computer has at least 400 MB of free storage space. Because Basic Tools uses Electron, it is not a small app (see why) and will take around 200-370-ish MB of your space. The benefit is that Basic Tools runs completely locally without internet connection, providing security, digital privacy, and a way to not rely everything on internet-based or AI-based tools.


In addition, because Basic Tools is a completely locally run app (due to the aforementioned reasons of security, privacy, and disconnection), there is no way for your downloaded app to automatically detect newly release updated. To keep up-to-date and enjoy the latest version of Basic Tools, please subscribe to this mailing list here.

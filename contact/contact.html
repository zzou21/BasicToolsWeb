How to Suggest A New Tool to be added to Basic Tools?

I always welcome new suggestions! The goal with Basic Tools is to make it a living app, where new functionalities are added whenever someone spots a need. To suggest one, you have multiple options:

  1. Email me at my email address above.
  2. Fill out the Google Form below.
  3. Engage in this online public forum (to be added).

You only need to do one of the above three. Regardless of which way you use to suggest a new tool, make sure that you articulate why your suggested new tool addresses an issue that is commonly seen and can serve the public (or a big population of a specialized community). In other words, make sure your tool suggestion addresses an issue that many people faces.

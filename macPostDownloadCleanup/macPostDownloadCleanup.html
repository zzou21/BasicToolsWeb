Mac Post-Download Clean Up

The .dmg File

For Mac computers, the way to download an app from the internet (and not from the App Store) is that the provider of your app (me) lets you download a .dmg file, which is Apple's proprietary disc format to provide security during software distribution. When you click on your .dmg file to download Basic Tools, the .dmg file and its accompanying disk mounter (which you can imagine it as plugging in a USB drive) will stay on your Desktop (or wherever on your computer that you downloaded them to), unless you delete them explicitly.
